Who We Are

A Legacy of
Trusted Trade

Founded in 2022 by Rediet Alemayehu, RVE Trading PLC was built with a clear vision — to bridge the trade gap between Ethiopia and global markets. In just four years, we have grown into a trusted import-export firm with over 20 active trade partners and $10M+ in annual trade volume.

We specialize in the sourcing, negotiation, logistics, and delivery of high-value commodities — serving governments, enterprises, and private clients with equal dedication.
